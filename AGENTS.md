## Requirements
- Make the minimum amount of change you need to fulfill all requirements. Do not over-design.
- Let the program crash naturally on unexpected errors.

## Prompting Guidance
- Keep prompts and instructions simple and direct.
- State constraints and success criteria explicitly.
- Use clear section headers or delimiters when injecting file content or other context.
- Prefer zero-shot instructions before adding examples.
- Avoid asking models to reveal chain-of-thought or hidden reasoning.
- Prefer pinned or snapshot model IDs for stable defaults when the provider supports them.
